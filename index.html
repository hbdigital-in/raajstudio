Our Story

Family. Passion. Photography.

Raaj Studio has seen the suburb of Mulund grow in front of its eyes. Located right opposite the Railway Station on the west, we carry a legacy built on a family's passion for photography.

Established over sixty years ago, our dedication to customer satisfaction has earned us an extensive following for creating the most imaginative, well-crafted photos and videos possible. Every wedding production reflects a unique personality and style for each couple we serve.

Tarun

Lead Photographer

The youngest of the lot, Tarun has always sought inspiration and challenge in variety — shooting extensively across travel, fashion, food, photojournalism, advertising, interiors, portraits, and automobiles. Wedding photography gets the best out of him and lets him capture special moments forever in his unique style.
